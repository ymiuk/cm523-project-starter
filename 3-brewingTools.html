Step 3: Brewing Tools

French Press
    Also known as a press pot or plunger pot, a French Press uses a cylindrical glass or stainless steel beaker and a plunger with a metal or nylon mesh filter to brew coffee. It produces a full-bodied and flavorful cup of coffee.
Drip Coffee Maker
    This is the most common coffee brewing method used in households. It uses a paper filter to drip hot water over ground coffee. It produces a clean and consistent cup of coffee.
Pour-over Coffee Maker
    This method uses a cone-shaped dripper with a paper filter to brew coffee. It requires pouring hot water over the ground coffee in a slow and steady stream. It produces a clean and bright cup of coffee.
Espresso Machine
    This brewing method uses high pressure to extract coffee from finely ground beans. It produces a concentrated shot of coffee with a creamy and rich flavor.
Cold Brew Maker
    This brewing method involves steeping coffee grounds in cold water for several hours to produce a smooth and less acidic cup of coffee. It's a great option for people who prefer iced coffee.
